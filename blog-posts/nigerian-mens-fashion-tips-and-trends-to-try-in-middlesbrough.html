Nigerian Men's Fashion: Tips and Trends to Try in Middlesbrough

  • Odeleke Janet
  • April 27th, 2023
  • 2 Comments

Nigeria is a country rich in culture and tradition, and this is evident in the way Nigerian men dress. From bold and vibrant prints to tailored suits, Nigerian men's fashion is diverse and versatile. If you're a Nigerian man living in Middlesbrough and you want to incorporate some of these styles into your wardrobe, then read on for our top tips and trends to try.

Ankara prints: Ankara is a popular African fabric that has been embraced by Nigerian men as a symbol of their cultural heritage. Incorporating Ankara prints into your wardrobe is a great way to add a pop of colour and pattern to your outfit. You can start with small accessories like pocket squares or ties, or go all out with a full Ankara suit.

Agbada: Agbada is a traditional Nigerian outfit that consists of a flowing robe worn over trousers and a shirt. It's a perfect outfit for special occasions and can be made from a variety of fabrics, from cotton to silk.

The Nigerian Kaftan: The Nigerian Kaftan is a stylish, versatile and comfortable outfit that can be dressed up or down. It's made from light cotton fabric and is perfect for hot weather. It can be worn with sandals for a casual look or dressed up with smart shoes for a more formal occasion.

The Nigerian Senator: The Nigerian Senator is a stylish and modern take on the traditional agbada. It's a fitted outfit that consists of a shirt, trousers and a matching jacket. It's perfect for a night out on the town or a formal event.

Caps and hats: Caps and hats are an essential accessory for any Nigerian man. They add a touch of style to any outfit and can be worn with both traditional and modern clothing. The traditional Nigerian cap, also known as the fila, is a great way to add a touch of cultural heritage to your outfit.

In conclusion, Nigerian men's fashion is rich and diverse, and there are many styles and trends to try. Incorporating these styles into your wardrobe is a great way to showcase your cultural heritage and stand out from the crowd. Whether you're attending a wedding, a party, or just going out for a night on the town, there's a Nigerian men's fashion style for every occasion.
