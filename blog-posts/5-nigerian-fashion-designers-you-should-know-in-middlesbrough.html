5 Nigerian Fashion Designers You Should Know in Middlesbrough

  • Odeleke Janet
  • April 17th, 2023
  • 2 Comments

Are you a Nigerian living in Middlesbrough and looking for Nigerian fashion designers to add to your wardrobe? Look no further than these five amazing Nigerian fashion designers making waves in Middlesbrough:

Lisa Folawiyo: Lisa Folawiyo is a Nigerian fashion designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. Her label, Jewel by Lisa, is known for its use of African prints and modern silhouettes. Her designs have been worn by many celebrities, including Beyoncé and Lupita Nyong'o.

Mai Atafo: Mai Atafo is a Nigerian fashion designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. His label, Mai Atafo, is known for its elegant and timeless designs. He has dressed many celebrities, including Rita Dominic and Stephanie Okereke.

Deola Sagoe: Deola Sagoe is a Nigerian fashion designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. Her label, Deola Sagoe, is known for its use of African prints and traditional embroidery techniques. Her designs have been worn by many international celebrities, including Oprah Winfrey and Will Smith.

Adebayo Oke-Lawal: Adebayo Oke-Lawal is a Nigerian fashion designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. His label, Orange Culture, is known for its androgynous and gender-fluid designs. His designs have been featured in many international fashion shows, including London Fashion Week.

Tiffany Amber: Tiffany Amber is a Nigerian fashion label founded by Folake Coker. The label is known for its use of African prints and modern silhouettes. Tiffany Amber has dressed many international celebrities, including Beyoncé and Kim Kardashian.

In conclusion, these five Nigerian fashion designers are making waves in Middlesbrough and should be on every Nigerian's radar. They all bring something unique to the fashion industry, whether it's their use of African prints or their gender-fluid designs. Support these designers and add a touch of Nigerian style to your wardrobe!
