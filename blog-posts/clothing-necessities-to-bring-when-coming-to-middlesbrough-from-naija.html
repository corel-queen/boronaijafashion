Clothing necessities to bring when coming to Middlesbrough from Naija

  • Odeleke Janet
  • May 1st, 2023
  • 3 Comments

Ah, the excitement of travelling to a new city! If you're a Nigerian coming to Middlesbrough for the first time, you're probably wondering what kind of clothes you should bring. Well, don't worry, we've got you covered! Here are the top clothing necessities you should bring when coming to Middlesbrough from Naija.


Winter Clothes - Middlesbrough can get quite chilly, especially during the winter months. So, make sure to pack warm clothes like coats, jackets, and sweaters. And don't forget your gloves and hats too!

Comfortable Shoes - Middlesbrough is a walkable city, so you'll be doing a lot of walking. Bring comfortable shoes like sneakers or boots that will keep your feet happy and comfy.


Traditional Nigerian Attire - Whether you're attending a Nigerian wedding or just want to show off your culture, it's always a good idea to bring traditional Nigerian attire. You can never go wrong with a beautiful Ankara dress or a perfectly tailored Agbada.


Formal Wear - If you're attending formal events like business meetings or conferences, it's important to bring some formal wear. A nice suit or a stylish dress will do the trick.


Casual Wear - Of course, you'll need casual clothes for everyday wear. Bring comfortable clothes like T-shirts, jeans, and shorts (for the summer months).


Accessories - Finally, don't forget to pack your accessories! Whether it's a stylish hat or a statement necklace, accessories can really make an outfit pop.


And there you have it, the top clothing necessities you should bring when coming to Middlesbrough from Naija. But remember, these are just suggestions. Bring whatever makes you feel comfortable and confident, and don't be afraid to show off your unique sense of style!
