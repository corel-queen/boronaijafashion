PRIVACY POLICY

Introduction

Thank you for visiting our website! At BoroNaija Fashion, we take your privacy seriously and want to provide you with transparent information about how we collect, use, and protect your personal data. This Privacy Policy applies to BoroNaija Fashion and all related services, features, and content (collectively, the "Service").

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information when you use our Service, including:

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and location when you sign up for our newsletter or participate in surveys or contests.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use our Service, including the pages you visit, the links you click, and the content you view.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access our Service, including your IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We may use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior and preferences.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our Service, as well as to personalize your experience and show you relevant ads. Specifically, we may use your information to:

  • Provide you with our newsletter, surveys, and other communications.
  • Understand how you use our Service and improve its content and functionality.
  • Personalize your experience by showing you relevant ads and content.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and the rights of others.

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with third-party service providers, such as ad networks and analytics providers, to help us provide and improve our Service. We may also share your information with our business partners and affiliates, but only as necessary to provide the Service to you. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Your Choices

You have certain choices about how we use your information. Specifically, you can:

  • Opt out of receiving our newsletter and other communications by following the unsubscribe link in the email.
  • Adjust your browser settings to reject cookies and similar technologies.
  • Adjust your ad preferences through the Digital Advertising Alliance or similar organizations.

Please note that if you opt out of receiving our communications or reject cookies and similar technologies, you may not be able to use certain features of our Service.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, and use. However, no system can guarantee 100% security, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, we will delete it as soon as possible.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or the law. If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at info@boronaijafashion.com.
